Chicken tendies

Description

Delicious and crispy fried chicken tenders that will melt in your mouth !


Ingredients (for 1 pers)

  • 200g chicken breasts
  • Some spices of your choice (paprika, oregano, pepper, salt etc.)
  • 1 egg
  • 10cl milk
  • 100g flour
  • 100g unsweetened cornflakes
  • Some frying oil
  • Mayonnaise, ketchup

Steps

  1. Start by cutting the chicken breasts into semi-fine strips.
  2. In a bowl, crack the egg, combine the milk and whisk.
  3. Put the cornflakes into a plastic bag and gently crack them with (something heavy enough to crush it) and put them in a bowl.
  4. In a separate bowl, combine the flour with the spices.
  5. Put the chicken into the flour first, then the egg mixture and the cornflakes. Repeat twice for extra crispiness.
  6. Once all of your chicken tendies are prepared, heat up a pot of oil on medium heat (just enough to cover the top of the semi-fine strips) to 170Â°C, if it's too hot reduce the heat.
  7. Cook the tendies until it is golden brown. This can take up from 7-15mn depending on the size of the pieces. Make sure to check and stir often so it doesn't end up burning / overcooking.
  8. Enjoy your tendies !
