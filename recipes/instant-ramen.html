Instant ramen

Description

Delicious, revisited instant ramen to make it even better !


Ingredients (for 1 pers)

  • 3 slices of pork or Bacon
  • Instant ramen packet
  • 1 egg
  • Some greens (cilantro, chives)
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Chili oil (optional)
  • Chicken stock (optional)
  • Pureed garlic (optional)

Steps

  1. Start by heating up some water in a pot.
  2. While the water is boiling, prepare the egg in a pan with sesame oil and fry until it's crispy on the outside.
  3. Remove the egg from the pan, cook the bacon strips with a tbsp of sesame oil and soy sauce, until it's crispy. Remove and reserve.
  4. Put the noodles in the boiling water and cook accordingly to the package instructions.
  5. While the noodles are cooking, cut the chives and cilantro.
  6. If you want more flavor in the broth, heat up some chicken stock along with half of the ramen's package seasoning sauce.
  7. Add the broth first, then noodles, egg, pork and greens. Optionally you can add a bit of chili oil and pureed garlic on the top for extra flavor.
  8. Enjoy your ramen!
